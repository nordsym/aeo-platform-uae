Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between SEO and AEO?
Traditional SEO is about ranking high in lists of blue links. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is about becoming the direct answer that AI models like ChatGPT and Claude provide.
How quickly will I see results?
AI engines often index faster than traditional crawlers. By strengthening your authority signals, most clients see measurable difference within 4 to 8 weeks.
Do I need to rebuild my website?
No. We optimize your existing architecture through technical layers like Schema.org and adjust the content structure so AI agents can more easily interpret your data.
What is GEO and why is it needed?
GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) focuses on building authority in generative search results like Google SGE. It's about becoming the preferred source when AI summarizes complex information.
